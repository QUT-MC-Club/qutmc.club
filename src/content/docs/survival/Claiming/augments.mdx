![alt text](../../../../assets/claim-augments.png)

## Augment Recipes and Effects

<CardGrid>
    <Card title="Ender Binding">
    ![Primitive Claim Anchor](../../../../assets/ender-binding.png)

    </Card>
    <Card title="Lake Spirits Grace">
    ![Reinforced Claim Anchor](../../../../assets/lake-spirits-grace.png)

    ![Glistening Claim Anchor](../../../../assets/angelic-aura.png)

    </Card>

    ![Crystal Claim Anchor](../../../../assets/village-core.png)

    <Card title="Withering Seal">
    ![Nethered Claim Anchor](../../../../assets/withering-seal.png)
    Prevents players from getting the wither status effect.

    <Card title="Chaos Zone">
    ![Nethered Claim Anchor](../../../../assets/chaos-zone.png)
