## Usage of Mod Launchers

Mod launchers are third party programs that simplify the usage of mods with Minecraft. 

Usage of Launchers should be realtively straightforward, most will isolate your instances as to not have conflicting mods or settings.
Modrinth and Prismlauncher both feature updaters to keep your modpacks working on the latest version.

Methods of usage vary between launcher so it is best to consult their official documentation.

[PrismLauncher Getting Started](https://prismlauncher.org/wiki/getting-started/)

Modrinth doesnt currently have any documentation for their launcher, if you have issues ask in their discord:

[Modrinth Discord](https://discord.com/invite/EUHuJHt)
